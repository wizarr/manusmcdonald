After two decades architecting commercial success from within, I deliberately pivoted. The corporate toolkit, while powerful, was no longer sufficient for the complexity of modern business problems. The true advantage is no longer found in managing processes, but in creating entirely new engines of value. This realization led me to pursue a rigorous MSc in Data Analytics, not as a career change, but as a deliberate acquisition of a creator's most powerful new medium: data.

Today, I operate as a multi-disciplinary Business Creator. I don't just analyze, I build. I don't just consult, I design. My work lives at the intersection of business process, data science, AI implementation, and serverless application development. I treat each business challenge as a creative problem, applying a fluid expertise to architect solutions that are not only effective but elegant and resilient.

Creation 1: The Knowledge Agent

The Business Problem: A client's senior team spent dozens of hours per week searching through a vast, unstructured library of internal documents to answer critical business questions, leading to slow decisions and duplicated effort.

My Creative & Technical Process: I designed and built a bespoke AI Knowledge Agent using Langchain and advanced LLMs. The process involved embedding the entire document library into a vector database and creating a secure, intuitive interface for natural language queries.

The Quantifiable Business Value: Reduced research time by 90%, unlocking an estimated €150k in annual productivity for the senior team.

[Simple UI Mockup of the Firebase App]

Creation 2: The Automated Claims Calculator

The Business Problem: A complex, manual spreadsheet-based process for calculating commercial claims was prone to human error, resulting in costly inaccuracies and compliance risks.

My Creative & Technical Process: I architected and deployed a secure, serverless web application using Firebase. The application codified the complex business logic into a reliable engine, providing a simple user interface for inputs and generating precise, auditable PDF reports.

The Quantifiable Business Value: Eliminated 100% of calculation errors and reduced the time-to-quote from 4 hours to 2 minutes.

[Before/After Process Flow Diagram]

Creation 3: The Commerce Engine

The Business Problem: A high-growth retail client's operational processes were buckling under a 400% increase in order volume, leading to shipping delays, inventory mismatches, and declining customer satisfaction.

My Creative & Technical Process: I conducted a full diagnostic of the end-to-end value chain, from procurement to fulfillment. I then designed and project-managed a complete process re-engineering, implementing a streamlined system for inventory management and order processing.

The Quantifiable Business Value: Increased fulfillment capacity by 500% with only a 20% increase in headcount, supporting sustained 400% YoY growth.
